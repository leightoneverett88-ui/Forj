Inside the platform

Coaching intelligence, not just charts.

The engines that turn your numbers into a plan you can run tomorrow.

01

Goal Engine

Pick a distance — mile to marathon — and the whole block rebuilds: every session, target pace and taper, programmed from your freshest race effort.

02

Real-Life Adaptation Engine

Seven signals — sleep, resting HR, stress, fuel, heat, life load and the time you've actually got — tune tomorrow's session to real life.

03

3D injury map

Tap where it hurts on a live 3D body. Recurrence detection, a return-to-run ladder and prehab are built in — before a niggle becomes a lay-off.

04

Race-pacing cards

Goal-aware splits, checkpoints and a red-line for race day. A Plan A and a Plan B, scaled to your distance, live on the start line.
